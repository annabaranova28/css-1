Minamata

2020

The 1970s. War photographer Eugene Smith, on assignment from Life magazine, returns to Japan, where an environmental disaster is unfolding in the small coastal town of Minamata. It is from there that Smith will make a report that will shake the whole world, take the greatest shots in the history of photography and go through a difficult path of overcoming and knowing himself.

The Minamata is one of Johnny Depp's most successful projects in recent years.
